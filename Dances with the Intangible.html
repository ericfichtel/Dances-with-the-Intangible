Title

in the lonely corners of the night
they hide in each shadow and lurk around each corner
i do not realize they are what i needed most
until they return for me

Too Fast
you say i move too fast
yet it feels like
i’ve been waiting here
in this vacuum of loneliness and weightless energy
forever
why is the hand moving so slow

feels like the faster i go
the more intense the suffering
oh please, cover my eyes
i’m too afraid to look at the clock
we are running out of time

i know you said
let’s meet at 8
im sorry

a german man, wrinkled and unheard at his death
told me that
you are an observer
that we are
relative
to one another
you're just particles to me now

yes we move at different speeds
but i never moved too fast to give you
the light
of day
you are not the person from my memories

finally
i have reached you
but you are not the same person from before
it feels like you are sitting right here
but where are you exactly

i still love your image
but that's all there is to love
the real you
whatever, whoever that is
i can't see that
we could have spent years together
but instead we chased away the days
